#1

We Clicked, Literally

We met on a dating app almost 2 years ago! She was afraid that he may be a serial killer. He was worried that she may be a widower with a child. But as fate would have it Rendy and Regina are just two boring people meant to be together.

#2

Hopeless Ramentic

We share a deep love of coffee, noodles and discounts. Our first date was at an old, legendary noodle joint in Central Jakarta. Cosmic forces were hard at work to show us this match was made in heaven, as we got away with free dinner that night.

#3

You Know It's Meant to Be When...

As time went on, we got to know each other’s quirks – in many ways we are polar opposites; but it may be said that the highlight of our relationship is that we keep learning from each other. On our last rowdy night out before the pandemic, one friend told us that it is such a beautiful thing to say about a relationship. We do not take this compliment lightly… you know who you are, thank you!

#4

Happy Beginning

Fast forward to January 2021, we are days away from being Mr & Mrs. Some say that marriage is a big leap, but we find that it is countless of incremental steps. The Bridezilla and The Groomster couldn’t have done this without you. Dear family and friends, please know that we are so grateful for all of your support and wishes!
